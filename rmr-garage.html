About RMR Garage

Founded in 2012 by racing enthusiast Roger M. Reynolds, RMR Garage has grown from a small workshop to one of the most respected custom bike builders in the industry.

Our team of engineers, fabricators, and designers live and breathe motorcycles. We combine racing technology with artistic craftsmanship to create bikes that perform as good as they look.

Every RMR build starts with a blank canvas and ends with a one-of-a-kind machine that reflects your personality and riding style.
